About CONSTRUCTION

CONSTRUCTION Villas and Apartments was established in 1995. CONSTRUCTION is one of the leading developers and trusted builders of villas and apartments in Kerala. Our Premium 3 BHK apartments, commercial and villa projects in Kochi, Kottayam, and Thiruvalla.“We have a commercial project, CONSTRUCTION Focus, in Seaport – Airport road near Infopark”, Kakkanad where office spaces are available for lease and rental. As Kochi is transforming into one among the metro cities in our country, a smart city has been developing at Kakkanad.“This is one of the reasons why our projects are mostly located at the heart of Kakkanad, Kochi.”



This compliments CONSTRUCTION’s philosophy of integrity, reliability, and impeccable service to customers. We believe in providing the best value for money in our commercial and residential projects with all luxury amenities.” CONSTRUCTION is now an established brand in luxury flats and villa projects in Kochi, when it comes to trust, reliability, and innovation. CONSTRUCTION intends to promote the concept of saving nature by focusing on green buildings and thereby sensitizing people to the need to conserve the environment and effective utilization of our natural resources.
